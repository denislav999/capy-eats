Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide and improve our services, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our platform.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly and cannot be disabled. They enable:

  • User authentication and account access
  • Shopping cart and checkout functionality
  • Security features and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing and performance optimization

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform by collecting anonymous information. They allow us to:

  • Count visits and traffic sources
  • Measure page performance
  • Identify popular content and features
  • Improve user experience based on usage patterns

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and preferences to provide enhanced features:

  • Saved delivery addresses and payment methods
  • Language and region preferences
  • Dietary restrictions and allergen warnings
  • Favorite kitchens and recent orders

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to show you relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns:

  • Personalized promotions and offers
  • Retargeting advertisements
  • Social media integration
  • Campaign performance tracking

You can opt out of marketing cookies through your cookie preferences or browser settings.

3. How Long Cookies Last

Cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted

4. Managing Cookies

You have several options to control cookies:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies
  • Cookie preferences: Use our cookie preference center to customize your settings
  • Opt-out tools: Visit industry opt-out pages for marketing cookies

Note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our platform and prevent you from using certain features.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use services from trusted third parties that may set their own cookies:

  • Analytics providers: Google Analytics, Mixpanel
  • Payment processors: Stripe, PayPal
  • Advertising platforms: Google Ads, Facebook Pixel
  • Support tools: Intercom, Zendesk

These cookies are governed by the respective third-party privacy policies.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our practices. We will notify you of significant changes through the platform or via email.

Questions About Cookies?

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@capyeats.com
