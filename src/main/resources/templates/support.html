Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I set up my kitchen profile?

After signing up, go to your dashboard and complete your kitchen profile with business details, menu, and photos.

What documents do I need to join?

You'll need a business license, food handler's certificate, and proof of insurance.

How long does approval take?

Kitchen approval typically takes 2-3 business days after submitting all required documents.

Orders & Payments

When do I receive payments?

Payments are processed weekly and deposited directly to your bank account.

What are the commission rates?

Our standard commission is 20%, with lower rates available for high-volume kitchens.

How do I handle order issues?

Contact support immediately for any order problems. We'll work with you to resolve issues quickly.

Technical Support

How do I update my menu?

Log into your dashboard and navigate to the Menu section to add, edit, or remove items.

Can I integrate with my POS system?

Yes, we offer API integration for popular POS systems. Contact our technical team for setup.

What if the app isn't working?

Try restarting the app first. If issues persist, contact our technical support team.
