About capyEats

Connecting food lovers with innovative ghost kitchens

Our Story

capyEats was born from a simple idea: great food shouldn't be complicated. We noticed that talented chefs were launching ghost kitchens, creating amazing dishes without the overhead of traditional restaurants. But customers had trouble discovering these hidden gems.

That's where we come in. We've built a platform that connects food lovers with the best ghost kitchens in their area. Our name comes from the capybara, nature's most chill and friendly animal, because we believe food delivery should be relaxed, reliable, and enjoyable.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to revolutionize food delivery by empowering ghost kitchens and delighting customers. We believe in:

  • Quality first: Curated kitchens that meet our high standards
  • Fair partnerships: Supporting small businesses and independent chefs
  • Innovation: Constantly improving the delivery experience
  • Community: Building connections between chefs and food lovers

Why Ghost Kitchens?

Ghost kitchens represent the future of food delivery. They allow talented chefs to focus on what they do best—creating incredible food—without the massive overhead of a traditional restaurant. This means better prices for you and more opportunities for culinary innovation.
